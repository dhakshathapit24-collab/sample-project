VIJAY

Basic Profile

  • Full Name: Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar

  • Screen Name: Vijay (often called Thalapathy Vijay)

  • Birth Date: 22 June 1974

  • Birth Place: Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India

  • Profession: Actor, Playback Singer, Dancer, Philanthropist, Politician

  • Industry: Tamil Cinema (Kollywood)


Early Life & Family

  • Vijay was born in Chennai into a film family — his father S. A. Chandrasekhar is a director and his mother Shoba Chandrasekhar is a playback singer and carnatic vocalist.

  • He began as a child artist in films directed by his father before moving into lead roles as a young actor.

Film Career

Early Career

  • Started as a child actor and then debuted as a lead actor in the early 1990s.

Breakthrough & Mass Success

  • Vijay became a major commercial star in Tamil cinema with hits through the 2000s and 2010s.
  • Some notable blockbusters include Ghilli (2004), Thuppakki (2012), Kaththi (2014), Mersal (2017), Master (2021), Leo (2023) and The Greatest of All Time (2024) — all of which were massive at the box office.
  • His films are known for mass appeal, strong dance numbers, punch dialogues, and action sequences, earning him a huge fan base.

Achievements

  • He’s one of the highest-paid actors in India and has several top-grossing films.
  • Vijay has received numerous awards and honors over his career for acting and social contributions.

Political Career (Since 2024)

Entry into Politics

  • Vijay formally entered politics on 2 February 2024 by launching his party Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK).
  • The party aims to contest the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly elections.

Background

  • Before this, he had a large fan welfare group called Vijay Makkal Iyakkam, active in social and local politics (including winning 115 of 169 seats in local body elections in 2021).

Retirement from Cinema

  • In February 2024, Vijay announced he would do only one more film before retiring from movies to focus fully on politics. That film — Jana Nayagan (Thalapathy 69) — is scheduled for release on 9 January 2026 (Pongal).

  • He completed shooting for Jana Nayagan in June 2025, marking the end of his film career.
